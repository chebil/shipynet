Welcome to SHIPYNET

SHIPYNET is a for-profit emerged corporation that operates in Transportation and Logistics area in Middle East region.
SHIPYNET adopts, integrates, and manages an intelligent cloud-based ride sourcing platform that provides on-demand truck service via smart devices.

Our target customers are individual and corporate clients who frequently use or supply Transport and Logistics services.

A win-win-strategy based-commission is charged for connecting customers (e.g., individuals or companies) with trucks drivers (e.g., subcontractors or transport carriers) via a cutting-edge intelligent application.

Our main strength is our ability to provide highly affordable (flat rate or subscriptions) goods transportation solutions.

We focuse on the cross-company empty truck rides minimization.

We follow the world's move toward Green Logistics requirements by reducing CO2 emissions.

About SHIPYNET

Mission Statement
The main mission of SHIPYNET is to provide affordable goods transportation solutions to customers via a cutting-edge cloud-based application. Under its win-win strategy-based slogan "Sharing Smart Saving", SHIPYNET smartly ensures a shareable profit by identifying, pricing, matching, and assigning given-up empty rides. SHIPYNET will follow the world's move toward Green Logistics requirements by reducing CO2 emissions.

Product
SHIPYNET is specialized in providing goods transportation and logistics services, and follows two business strategies to share services under a progressively-built business network to ensure profitable cooperation. Firstly, SHIPYNET offers a unique showcase platform for transport and logistics services suppliers (transport carriers or subcontractors) to exhibit their whole services for customers (companies or individuals). Secondly, SHIPYNET provides direct end-user services to customers by suggesting the more profitable and adequate available services according to their requests and profiles. At these centers, customers will be able to make highly affordable deals with adequate suppliers.

Market
SHIPYNET addresses all worldwide companies and individuals, that need goods transportation services, and offers a bridge (link) with suppliers that provide these services. SHIPYNET platform provides an online sharing of transportation and logistics services and will focus on the empty rides generally given-up by suppliers. SHIPYNET have an open business network-built strategy without setting boundaries limit in terms of customers and suppliers sizes, locations, nationalities. For efficiency and trustworthy purposes, an accurate assessment system will is used by SHIPYNET to evaluate and rank customers and suppliers that present the main platform users.
